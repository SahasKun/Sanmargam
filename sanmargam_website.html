About the Organization

US Kids for Water is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing clean water access to children in underserved communities. Founded by young activists, we believe that every child deserves access to safe, clean drinking water.

Our Mission

We work with local communities and partners to build sustainable water infrastructure, educate about water conservation, and advocate for policies that protect water resources for future generations.
